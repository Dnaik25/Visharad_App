Class 24
======

Satsang Diksha Shlok 90-91
---------------------
 - Mandirāņām hi nirmāņam tad-āgnām-anusrutya cha I Divyānām kriyate bhaktyā sarva-kalyāņa-hetunā ॥90॥
 - Purushottama-mūrtyā tad-madhya-khande yathā-vidhi Sahitam sthāpyate mūrtir-Aksharasyā'pi Brahmaņaha ॥91॥
 - Te āgnāne anusarīne sarvanu kalyān thāy te hetuthī divya mandironu nirmān bhakti-bhāvthī karavāmā āve chhe ane tenā madhya-khandmā Purushottam Bhagwānnī mūrtinī sāthe Aksharbrahmanī mūrti pan vidhivat sthāpavāmā āve chhe. (90-91)

Satsang Diksha Shlok 92
---------------------
 - Evam eva gruhādyeshu kruteshu mandireshvapi Madhye prasthāpyate nityam sā'ksharah Purushottamaha ॥92॥
 - E ja rīte ghar ādi sthalone vishe karel mandiromā paņ madhyamā hammeshā Akşharbrahma sahit Purushottam Bhagwanne prasthāpit karavāmā āve chhe. (92)

Satsang Diksha Shlok 93
---------------------
 - Prātah sayam yathā-kālam sarva-satsangibhir janaihi | Nikațam mandiram gamyam bhaktyā darshāya pratyaham ॥93॥
 - Sarve satsangīoe savāre, sānje athavā potānā anukūļ samaye pratidin bhaktie karīne samīpe āvel mandire darshane javu. (93)

Satsang Diksha Shlok 94
---------------------
 - Yathā sva-dharma-rakshā syāt tathaiva vastra-dhāraņam | Satsangi-nara-nārībhih karaṇīyam hi sarvadā ॥94॥
 - Sarve satsangī nar- nārīoe sadāy je rīte potānā dharmanī rakshā thāya te ja rīte vastro dhāravā. (94)

Satsang Diksha Shlok 95
---------------------
 - Satsanga-drudhatārtham hi sabhārtham antike sthitam I Gantavyam prati-saptāham mandiram vā'pi mandalam ॥95॥
 - Satsangnī drudhatā māte dar athavādiye samīp āvel mandirmā ke mandalmā sabhā bharavā javu. (95)
