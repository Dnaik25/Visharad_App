Class 10
======

Satsang Diksha Shlok 23
---------------------
 - Nottamo nirvikalpash-cha nishchayah Paramātmanaha । Na svātma-brahma-bhāvo’pi Brahmā’kṣharam gurum vinā ॥ 23 ॥
 - Akṣharbrahma guru vinā Paramātmāno uttam nirvikalp nishchay na thaī shake tathā potānā ātmāne viṣhe brahmabhāv paṇ prāpta na thaī shake. (23)

Vachanamrut
-----------
• Vach.Ga.An.27
  “shāstre kahyā je niṣhkām, nirlobh, nirmān, nihsvād, nihsneh ityādik santnā lakṣhaṇ tene sāmbhaḷīne evā lakṣhaṇ jyā dekhāy evā je sant tene ne Bhagwānne sākṣhāt sambandh hoy; māṭe evā santnā vachane karīne Bhagwānno nishchay karavo ne tenā vachanmā draḍh vishvās karavo tene nishchay kahīe.”

• Vach.Lo.12
  “aṣhṭāvarṇe yukta evā je koṭi koṭi brahmānḍ te je Akṣharne viṣhe aṇunī peṭhe jaṇāy chhe, evu je Puruṣhottam Nārāyaṇnu dhāmrūp Akṣhar te rūpe pote rahyo thako Puruṣhottamnī upāsanā kare, tene uttam nirvikalp nishchayvāḷo kahīe.”

Kirtan
-----------------------
• Sadguru Premanand Swami
  Nirvikalp uttam ati, nischay tav Ghanshyām;

  Albelā tam āgḷe, hu araj karū e sārū... e 2

Vachanamrut
-----------
• Vach.Lo.7
  “je brahmarūp thayo tene ja Puruṣhottamnī bhaktino adhikār chhe.”

• Vach.Ga.Ma.62
  “potānā ātmānu brahmarūpe darshan thāy chhe ne te brahmane viṣhe Parabrahma je Nārāyaṇ tenu paṇ darshan thāy chhe. Ane te darshannā karanārāne em anubhav thāy chhe je, ‘Hu ātmā chhu ane māre viṣhe je Paramātmā chhe te akhanḍ rahyā chhe.’ Ane evī rītnī akhanḍ sthiti rahe e ātmaniṣhṭhānī ati uttam dashā chhe.”

• Vach.Ga.Pa.40
  “Akṣharbrahmanā sādharmyapaṇāne pāmīne kevaḷ Bhagwānnī mūrtine viṣhe ja nimagna raheto hoy tene nirvikalp samādhivāḷo kahīe.”

• Vach.Ga.Pa.60
  “jene ekāntiknā dharmamā sthiti thaī hoy te thakī ja ekāntikno dharma pamāy chhe.”

• Vach.Ga.Pa.54
  “Swadharma, gnān, vairāgya ane māhātmyagnān teṇe sahit je Bhagwānnī bhakti teṇe yukta evā je Bhagwānnā Ekāntik Sādhu tenā prasang thakī Bhāgwat Dharmanu poṣhaṇ thāy chhe ane vaḷī jīvne mokṣhanu je dvār te paṇ evā Sādhunā prasang thakī ughāḍu thāy chhe.”

Swamini Vato
------------
• Swa.Vato.1/119
  “Koṭi janme kasar ṭaḷavānī hoy te āj ṭaḷī jāy ne brahmarūp karī mūke, jo kharekharā sādhu maḷe ne te kahe tem kare to.”
