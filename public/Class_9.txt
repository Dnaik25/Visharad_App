Class 9
======

Satsang Diksha Shlok 20
---------------------
 - Āshrayet Sahajānandam Harim Brahmā’kṣharam tathā । Guṇātītam gurum prītyā mumukṣhuh svātma-muktaye ॥ 20 ॥
 - Mumukṣhu potānā ātmānī mukti māṭe Sahajānand Shrīhari tathā Akṣharbrahma swarūp guṇātīt guruno prītie karīne āsharo kare. (20)

Vachanamrut
-----------
• Vach.Je.1
  “māyā taravāno upāy e chhe je, jyāre sarva karma ane māyā teno nāsh karanārā ne māyāthī par je sākṣhātkār Shrī Puruṣhottam Bhagwān athavā te Bhagwānnā maḷel sant temanī jīvne prāpti jyāre thāy chhe tyāre temanā āshraythī māyā ullanghāy chhe.”

• Vach.Ga.Ma.59
  “Bhagwān ne Bhagwānnā sant e ja kalyāṇkārī chhe.”

• Vach.Lo.3
  “Jene Bhagwānno ne santno māhātmya-gnāne sahit nishchay hoy...”

• Vach.Ga.Ma.59
  “Bhagwānnā santnī sevā to bahu moṭā puṇyavāḷāne maḷe.”

• Vach.Va.14
  “...satpuruṣhno āsharo kare tyāre game tevo pāpī hoy to paṇ ati pavitra thaī jāy chhe...”

Swamini Vato
------------
• Swa.Vato.1/114
  “Bhagwān ane Moṭā Sādhune āshare karīne to vādaḷ jevā dukh āvavānā hoy te paṇ ṭaḷī jāy ne sādhan karīne to kūṭī kūṭīne marī jāy to paṇ na ṭaḷe.”
